Version: 5.0.0
Date: 12. 18. 2021
  Changes:
    - First release by robot256.
    - Incorporated new sounds for steam trains, boats and ships. Currently supports Yuoki Industries, Steam Locomotive, and Cargo Ships.
    - More settings to control sound triggers based on type of vehicle.  NOTE: Existing settings will be erased when upgrading.
    - Other mods to add custom locomotive sounds during the data stage.
    - Train sounds can now be used in the Programmable Speaker.
    - Please report any bugs, incompatibilities, or ideas for more sounds!
